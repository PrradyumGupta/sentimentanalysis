📖 What About SentiMenti Analyzer?

SentiMenti Analyzer is a powerful sentiment analysis tool that helps users analyze the tone and emotions behind their text. Whether for social media monitoring, customer feedback, or personal projects, our AI-driven model provides accurate results.

🔹 How It Works?

Our system processes your input text and classifies the sentiment into categories like Positive, Neutral, or Negative using Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Machine Learning.
